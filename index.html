#cancelcovid

Let’s do something about COVID-19, notoriously known as coronavirus. Other countries have been successful by limiting the spread of the virus by enacting quarantine measures. By reducing the number of cases, we can save millions of lives including the most at-risk members of our population: the immunocompromised, the elderly, and infants. We need the United States to take these measures, and we the people need to demand it.

What are quarantine measures?

Limiting social interaction as much as possible! Quarantine measures include prohibiting people from going to school, workplaces, public gatherings, and any other unnecessary travel. Visit Staythefuckhome.com for self-quarantine tips.

Quarantine is effective!

After China implemented its quarantine, new cases of COVID-19 dropped significantly. Japan and South Korea have also seen success in lowering the rate of infection. Marc Bevand is updating charts on COVID-19 case reports globally on his Twitter account: @zorinaq

It is important!

While the spread of the virus is inevitable, slowing down the spread is important to prevent overwhelming hospital resources. If too many people flood hospitals, people who would have otherwise recovered quickly, have to wait longer to receive care. By limiting the spread over time, we can significantly reduce the number of lives impacted.

This is known as flattening the curve.

You can help!

Contact your government representatives and tell them to take action! Below is a sample letter and instructions on how to find and contact your representatives.

Contact your representatives.

Visit myreps.datamade.us or commoncause.org to look up your congressperson, both of your senators, as well as your state senators and representatives. Make your voice heard! Call your representatives!

What to say:

Hello Senator/Congressperson [Name], in order to reduce the spread of COVID-19, I urge you to help enact quarantine measures, the faster we act, the more lives we can save.

It is critical and time-sensitive to enact the following:
  • Close schools, workplaces, prohibit public gatherings and ban any unnecessary travel.
  • Free and accessible testing and treatment for Coronavirus
  • Close down all bars, dine-in restaurants, gyms, and theaters nationwide
  • Ask for and accept help from the Chinese government and/or any outside government who has successfully battled the pandemic
  • Impose eviction moratoriums nationwide
  • Nationwide rent freezes
  • A medium-term relief solution to offset a pending ripple of missed rent/mortgage payments
  • Paid sick leave for all workers
  • Emergency Universal/Unconditional Basic Income
